ABOUT US

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by master baker Sarah Johnson, Let It Bake began as a small neighborhood bakery with a passion for authentic, handcrafted breads and pastries.

What started as a humble storefront has grown into a beloved local institution, known for our commitment to traditional techniques and the finest ingredients. We still bake everything in-house daily, just like we did on day one.

Our team of skilled bakers brings decades of combined experience, blending time-honored methods with creative innovation to deliver exceptional quality in every bite.
